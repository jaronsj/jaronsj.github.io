Target Audience

Description

The intended target audience for this site is those that are wanting to plan a camping trip. During the summer the weather can pretty fast and campers would want to know what kind of items they would need to pack. This website would help them to plan their trips.

Personas

Randy: My name is Randy and I love to be spending time outdoors. The weather plays a big part in what I pack when I want to go camping. It would be useful to have a website that can help me prepare for my trip. I want to be prepared!

Kailey: My name is Kailey. I grew up camping and being outdoors. I hate it when my camping trip gets ruined by some bad weather! It would be nice to be able to go camping while I am in Idaho. I have noticed that the weather can change several times in a day. A website that can tell me the perfect time to go camping would be great!

Scenarios

A company wants to go on a company retreat into the mountains, but they need to be able to plan their retreat before thy take everyone to the mountains.

A family of 5 wants to go camping for the weekend, and enjoy each others company. They would need to be able to tell the weather so that everyone can be packed and prepared.

A college FHE group want to go for on a cookout in nature and be able to learn more about each other. Being able to tell what the weather would be like would allow them to plan not only the day, but also the time they should go.

A person that is tired of being indoors due to COVID-19 wants to get outside. They would be able to plan their escape from indoors and get some fresh air.

An older couple that travel across the country for their retirement wants to stop and take a break from the road. When they can see the weather in the local area they would be likely to stop, and enjoy the sunshine.

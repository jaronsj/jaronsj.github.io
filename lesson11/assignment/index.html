About this Website:

The purpose of this website is to supply you with the much needed information of the weather, while you are planning your camping trip! There is no worse thing than planning a camping trip and have the weather ruin your weekend. With Camper's Perfect Weather you can take that guessing out of your weekend. You will be able to plan your trip in peace, and not worry about getting rained out!
